### Introduction
You are being asked to participate in a research study. Before you agree, however, you must be fully informed about the purpose of the study, the procedures to be followed, and any benefits, risks, or discomfort that you may experience as a result of your participation. This form presents information about the study so that you may make a fully informed decision regarding your participation.

### Purpose
The purpose of this study is to compile a set of user interface (UI) design characteristics that are engaging and locally relevant. Additionally, this study aims to identify key features that drive the use of online academic resources.

### Procedures to be Followed
The following survey is divided into two sections: UI design choices and features of online academic resources.

1.  **Section 1:** Participants will be asked to choose between two UI design options, selecting the one that is most appealing and easy to understand. After making a selection, participants will have the option to briefly explain their choice. This explanation is optional.
2.  **Section 2:** Participants will rank the effectiveness of a given feature on a scale of 1-5 stars. If a feature is rated 1 or 5 stars, participants can optionally explain their choice.

Lastly, participants will be able to answer an open-ended question about other e-learning resources. This survey should take no more than 10 minutes to complete.

### Risks to Study Participants
There are no foreseeable risks or discomfort that participants should experience.

### Benefits to Research Participants and Others
The results of this survey will be used to improve the #TeamGraduate online learning platform and provide additional educational resources to Namibian students.

### Record Keeping and Confidentiality
All data collected during this project will be kept confidential in a Private Google Drive, accessible only to the members of the IQP project, project advisors, and team sponsor: Helena Mboti. Additionally, participants will remain anonymous in the results of our project. Emails will only be collected if participants choose to enter a raffle.
